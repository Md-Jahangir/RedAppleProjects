How To Play

WIN Pick 1 Player and finish 1st to win
PLACE Pick 1 Player which finishes either 1st or 2nd (total Players under 7)
SHOW Pick 1 Player which finishes either 1st or 2nd (total Players over 7)
SWINGER Pick 2 Players which finishes among top 3, no order
QUINELLA Pick 2 Players and finish 1st or 2nd, in either order, to win
EXACTA Pick 2 Players and finish 1st and 2nd, in exact order, to win
TRIO Pick 3 Players and finish among 1st, 2nd, 3rd, in any order, to win
TRIFECTA Pick 3 Players and finish 1st, 2nd and 3rd, in exact order, to win
HIGH LOW Pick 1 and finish 1st to win
ODD EVEN Pick 1 and finish 1st to win
1. SELECT THE RACE START TIME

*** The match session shown in the guide is the current betting session.

* If this round and the round of theodds table are different, move the mouse at the time of the relevant round and click to enter that round. It will be changed to the appropriate odds table.

* To view the odds table for the relevant round, move the mouse to the red select bar and click on the relevant time.

2. SELECT THE BETTING TYPE

Select the Betting Type by moving a mouse and click on the Betting Type Tap.

3. SELECT THE BETTING METHOD

There are 2 ways to bet.

Betting Method [1]

Click the odds on the odds table after reviewing all odds.

The number in the vertical line represents a player which comes in First.

Betting Method [2]

After reviewing odds, select a player to finish in first, then another player for the second place.

You can select multiple players for the second place.

4. SELECT BET SLIP AMOUNT AND BUY BET SLIP
Select BET SLIP AMOUNT from the tab above and click "BUY BET SLIP".
Click "RESET" button to reset BET SLIP AMOUNT if you make an error setting up BET SLIP AMOUNT.

You can selet many tabs to create an amount not on the tab menu above.

(For example, you can buy 20K by clicking 10K twice.)

5. BUY BET SLIP

The Bet Slips will display all bet details after clicking BUY BET SLIP.

Review the confirmed Bet Slips and click X to remove any Bet Slips if you change your mind.

Click PLACE BET to execute the purchase of all Bet Slips confirmed and displayed.

6. BET SLIP PURCHASE AND BET CONFIRMATION

After PLACE BET is confirmed, the purchsed Bet slips are displayed on the right screen.

7. SOUND VOLUME AND STREAMING SCREEN SIZE CHANGE

The default sound setting is mute. To turn on the sound, click the speaker icon at the bottom left of the screen

The sound volume can be adjusted on the bar on the right side of the speaker.

To separate the screen and change the screen size to the desired size, select the box on the left of the boxes in the bottom right to separate the screen, then just adjust the screen size.

To view it in full screen, click on the box on the far right of the boxes in the lower right corner in the lower right corner to change to full screen.

To return to the original state, press the "ESC" key on your keyboard.
