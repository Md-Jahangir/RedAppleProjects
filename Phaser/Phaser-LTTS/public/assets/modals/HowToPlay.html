HOW TO PLAY

These are the main interactions needed for this challenge:
  1. 'P' lets you pick up objects. These will appear in your item box at the bottom of your screen.
  2. 'Direction keys' allow you to move around the map.
  3. 'Spacebar' lets you use or view objects in your item box or interact with the environment, e.g. doors and terminals.
  4. 'L' lets you leave objects. You can only have one item in your item box at one time, so must leave something to pick another thing up.
  5. 'Enter' lets you send global messagees to all players.

You can open this pop-up again at any time by pressing 'I'.
