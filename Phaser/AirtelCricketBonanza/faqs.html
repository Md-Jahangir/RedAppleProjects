FAQS

How can I register in Airtel World Cup Campaign?

You can register in Airtel World Cup Campaign by visiting the Airtel Thanks
App

  • Click on Airtel Cricket Bonanza banner or button
  • Register with your playername
  • Click on submit to complete the registration
Who all can participate in the Campaign?

All Airtel Thanks App customers can participate in the Campaign

Can I participate daily in the Campaign?
Yes, you can participate everyday in the campaign
How can I score runs?

You can play a maximum of 2 overs in a day, and the runs you make in these overs will keep accumulating across days. If you have claimed a reward the corresponding number of runs will be reduced from your cumulative score.

In the match, you have to tap at the right moment and direction to score maximum runs. You can get better at it with practice!

How can I claim rewards?

You can claim rewards against the runs scored by you. Once you claim a reward the runs corresponding to the reward will get deducted. You can claim one type of reward only once. However, you can claim another type of reward on being eligible for it.

What is the Campaign duration?

This offer is valid for Airtel Thanks App Customers, between 10th October 2019 to 09th November 2019.

How will the Top 10 Winners be selected?

At the end of the campaign the top 10 players with maximum runs scored will be declared as the winners. The top 10 scorers at any point of time is visible from the dashboard under the Top scorers tab

When will I receive my gifts?

Your gift will be dispatched within 5-6 working days from the date of winning. You will be contacted by email or SMS regarding the same.

What is the practice mode?

You can play as many overs as you want to in this mode. Your score will not be altered. You can get better at timing and direction to score more when you play the actual match.
